Step 1

Visit the Stream registration page

Tap any “Register” button on this site (or use the official link). Create your account with your correct details — this is what opens your Stream dashboard.

Step 3

Make payment & get activated

After choosing your package, make payment using the official payment details shown on the registration page. Then submit your payment proof for confirmation so your Stream account can be activated.

  • Send a clear screenshot/receipt showing amount, date, and sender name
  • Include your Stream account email/phone (the one you registered with)
  • Mention the package you paid for: Plus or Ultra
