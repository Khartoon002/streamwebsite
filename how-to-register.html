Step-by-step

How to Register for Stream

Follow these steps to create your account, verify your email, complete onboarding, and access your dashboard.

Go to Sign Up Back to Home

1) Create your account

Open the signup page and enter your details. Use a valid email address you can access right now.

Open Sign Up →

2) Verify your email

Check your inbox for the verification email. Click the link inside to confirm your account.

Tip: Check spam/junk if you don’t see it within a minute.

3) Complete onboarding

Follow the on-screen prompts to finalize your profile. Keep it accurate for smoother access.

4) Access your dashboard

Once onboarding is complete, you can log in and start using Stream immediately.

Common questions

How long does Stream registration take?

Usually 2–5 minutes, depending on email delivery time.

What if I don’t receive the verification email?

Check spam/junk folders, confirm you typed your email correctly, then try again or request a resend.

Can I register from my phone?

Yes. The Stream registration flow is mobile-first and tap-friendly.
