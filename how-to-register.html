How to register on Stream

Follow these simple steps to join the Stream community and start your journey.

Step 1

Create your account

Open the signup page and fill your details correctly (name, phone, email). Make sure all information is accurate.

Go to signup
Step 2

Choose a plan

Pick Plus or Ultra based on your needs. You can start with the free tier and upgrade anytime.

See payment options
Step 3

Make payment

Pay using the listed methods. Use the exact details shown on the payments page for smooth processing.

Step 4

Submit proof

After payment, submit your proof for confirmation. Keep the screenshot clear and include all details.

Step 5

Get activated

Once verified, your account unlocks the features in your chosen plan. Welcome to Stream!

Pro tips to avoid delays

  • Use your real name For matching payment records and verification.
  • Clear screenshot required Show amount, sender name, date, and transaction ID.
  • Contact support if needed Message support with your email/phone for assistance.
